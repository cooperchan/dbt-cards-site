This site, inspired by the work of Dr. Marsha Linehan (creator of Dialectical Behavior Therapy), was built by one person: from coding and design to drawing the intro illustrations and writing the flashcard content (with the exception of icons, which were sourced using system emojis, Font Awesome, and Canva). It was created on a shoestring budget as a personal passion project, with the goal of making DBT skills easier to explore and practice, for free.
